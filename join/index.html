50th Kentucky Wing Squadron

So you want to be a cadet?

Cadet life is an experience, so prospective cadets must visit their local squadron in-person three times before making the decision to join. We meet at the Kentucky National Guard building in Walton, KY every Monday beginning at 6:30 pm. Click below to see a map of the area!

Locate

I've visited three times... Now what?

After the third visit to your local squadron, the prospective cadet and their parent(s) must meet with the squadron commander before membership to the Civil Air Patrol can proceed. This meeting is to ensure that both the prospective cadet and their parent(s) fully understand the commitment that the prospective cadet is undertaking and exactly what will be expected of them.

Valid proof of the prospective cadet’s identity must be provided to the squadron commander during your meeting. The following are acceptable:

  • U.S. Passport
  • Permanent Resident Card (I-551)
  • Certified copy of Birth Certificate
  • Social Security Card
  • Drivers License or State Issued ID
  • Other I-9 approved documentation

Applying for membership.

After the meeting with the local squadron commander, prospective cadets may apply for membership online, and then local squadron leadership may approve the application.

To submit an application for cadet membership, visit the Online Cadet Membership Application page by clicking below. Youth will need a parent or guardian at their side as they complete the online form and make payment via credit card. To apply for cadet membership online, our Wing is KY and our Unit is 050. Please review information appropriately!

Watch your email!

Check your e-mail for a note confirming your membership before the next meeting. If your squadron denies your membership application for some reason, you’ll receive word via e-mail and your credit card will be refunded. Check out the new Cadet Guide below once accepted.
