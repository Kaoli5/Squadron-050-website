50th Kentucky Wing Squadron

Leadership

The civil air patrol values leadership. We derrive our values of leadership from the Air force. Each cadet gains knowledge in leadership through group activities or classroom time. Eventually, each cadet learns to lead a small team, manage projects, and think independently.

Aerospace Education

Continuing its long tradition, Civil air patrol aims to educate youth and adults alike on aviation and aerospace science. CAP aims to get youth interested in Stem carreers by providing various diverse programs.

Aerospace Education

The CAP AE Program initiatives introduce a diverse population of students and adults to Aerospace as a pathway toward the development of a patriotic and technologically-prepared future workforce.

Civil air patrols AE Program is important for kids and adults alike. It makes kids extremely interested in AE, and also helps fund the program as a whole.

Emergency Services

Civil air patrol cadets conducts various activities to serve in times of crisis. Civil air patrol participates in Search and Rescue, keeps and maintains Homeland Security, provides Disaster relief, and promotes a drug-free lifestyle.

Is being a cadet right for me?

Civil air patrol was initially founded on december 1st, 1941, on the basis of allowing patriotic citizens to serve their country when they couldn't enlist. Do you want to serve your community? Are you interested in aviation, search and rescue, or the Military in general? Then CAP Just might be a great fit for you!
